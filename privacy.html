3. How CodX Editor Works

CodX Editor is a completely client-side application that runs entirely in your web browser. Here's what that means for your privacy:

3.1 Local Storage Only

All your code, projects, and settings are stored locally in your browser's storage (localStorage). This means your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

3.2 No Server Communication

When you write code in CodX Editor, it's processed entirely on your device. We don't send your code to any server for processing, analysis, or storage. Everything happens locally in your browser.

3.3 No Third-Party Scripts

CodX Editor doesn't load third-party tracking scripts, advertising networks, or analytics tools. The only external resources we load are open-source libraries (like code highlighting tools) that are necessary for the editor to function, and these don't collect any data about you.

4. Your Data Stays With You

Since CodX Editor stores everything locally in your browser:

  • Your projects remain on your device
  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can clear your data at any time through your browser settings
  • If you clear your browser data, your projects will be deleted (so make sure to export important work!)

5. Optional Features

CodX Editor may offer optional features like sharing or collaboration. If you choose to use these features:

  • You will be clearly informed before any data is shared
  • You maintain full control over what you share
  • Sharing is always opt-in, never automatic
  • We still don't collect or store your personal information
